**<:protip:1368106561045659678> How to Record Gameplay/Desktop on a Mac**

**1. QuickTime Player (NO AUDIO)**
Open QuickTime Player and go to File
Select New Screen Recording or press `⌘ + ⌃ + N`
If you have multiple displays, choose which one to record
Press `⌘ + ⌃ + Esc` to stop recording

**2. Quick Recorder App (YES AUDIO)**
A free, open-source, light-weight, powerful screen recorder for macOS.
Download [Quick Recorder↗](https://lihaoyun6.github.io/quickrecorder/)
