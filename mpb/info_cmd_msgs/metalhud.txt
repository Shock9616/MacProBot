**<:protip:1368106561045659678> How to Enable the Metal HUD on Mac**
IMPORTANT: Make sure both your launcher (Steam, CrossOver, etc.) and your game are fully closed before enabling or disabling the HUD.

**Terminal Commands**

Enable Metal HUD:
```/bin/launchctl setenv MTL_HUD_ENABLED 1```

Disable Metal HUD:
```/bin/launchctl unsetenv MTL_HUD_ENABLED```

**MetalHUDMenu Menu Bar App**

We highly recommend using MetalHUDMenu to enable and customize the Metal HUD system wide!
[**MetalHUDMenu ↗**](<https://github.com/Jfishin/MetalHUDmenu>)
[**Guide ↗**](<https://youtu.be/SDs3xhA2Ufo>)

**Metal HUD Keyboard Shortcuts**

`Fn + Shift + F9` to show/hide the Metal HUD once enabled
`Fn + Shift + F7` to move the Metal HUD around the screen
`Fn + Shift + F12` to customize the HUD

<:protip:1368106561045659678> Pro Tip: You can triple click the HUD to get the customization controls!
